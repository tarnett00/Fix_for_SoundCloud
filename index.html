Welcome to Fix for SoundCloud

This is a simple javascript bookmarklet to resolve the issue with seeking / skipping forwards and backwards with the mouse. Simply add to your bookmarks bar and click to remedy.

You can download the automatic extension/fix here: Chrome Extension - Fix for SoundCloud
